CYRUS MOTORSPORTS AJCE

Cyrus Motorsports AJCE is a students' formula racing team of Amal Jyothi College of Engineering established under Club TORERO. The team aims to take part in National and International events such as the Society of Automotive Engineers Supra 2023 (SAE Supra 2023), Formula Bharath and many other formula students racing events across India for the season 2023. The team designs, fabricates and tests formula-style race cars which gives students a chance to apply real-life engineering technologies. Formula student events provide a platform for engineering students to ideate, design, and select components by following strict SAE regulations which give . CYRUS MOTORSPORTS AJCE SAE SUPRA:SUPRA is organized annually by SAEINDIA with the support of Maruti Suzuki. The event provides a platform for students to apply their engineering skills to design and construct a Formula category vehicle as per defined performance and safety specifications.

Club TORERO

TORERO, The Technical Club of Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, Kanjirapally is a collective of students and faculty of the college aimed to grant students the possibility to partake in multiple national and international events organized by expert associations like SAE, ASME, etc. In addition, the club aspires to conduct technical workshops, talks, classes and hands-on training on modern engineering advancements. The club produced teams to participate in Tractor Design Competition TDC SAE, Avian SAE Aerothon, and M Baja.
